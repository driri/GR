About

Dr. Ganit Richter is Academic Head of the Management Information Systems Department at the College of Management Academic Studies. She is also a lecturer in Management Information Systems at the College of Management Academic Studies and an adjunct faculty member in Information and Knowledge Management at the University of Haifa.

Her academic background includes a Ph.D. in Information and Knowledge Management from the University of Haifa, a post-doctoral fellowship at the Technion - Israel Institute of Technology, and earlier studies in information and knowledge management, non-profit management, pure mathematics, mathematics, and fine arts.

Her work combines experimental research, interactive platforms, and behavioral trace data analysis to examine how people interpret information, evaluate digital content, and respond to systems designed to support judgment, participation, and learning.

Research Interests

Human-AI Interaction

Public engagement with AI-generated content, algorithmic mediation, trust, doubt, and credibility.

Misinformation & Visual Trust

How people assess authentic and AI-generated visuals under conditions of uncertainty.

Gamification & Incentive Design

Game-based systems, rewards, scoring mechanisms, and their effects on motivation and knowledge contribution.

Knowledge Management

Digital knowledge creation, sharing, participation, and the design of knowledge environments.

Learning & Digital Pedagogy

AI, algorithmic bias, ethics, and the use of interactive systems in higher education.

Methods

Experimental design, behavioral trace data analysis, and research through interactive platforms.

Current Projects & Research Tools

FakeBuster

A gamified “social vaccination” platform that generates behavioral datasets on how people interpret AI-generated imagery.

Detecting AI-Generated Visuals in Biometric Contexts

A funded project supported by the Center for Cyber, Law and Policy and the National Cyber Directorate examining detection of AI-generated visuals in biometric contexts.

Algorithmic Truth Labels and GenAI Content Detection

A research project on the effect of algorithmic truth labels on human detection of generative AI content.

GenAI Visual Misinformation & Social Inoculation

A Digital Resilience Initiative project on generative AI visual misinformation and social inoculation.

Tour It Yourself / TIY

A gamified augmented reality interactive map for studying information value from multiple perspectives.

WhatsApp Status Longitudinal Corpus

A longitudinal dataset of everyday visual communication for studying collective meaning-making and digital memory over time.
